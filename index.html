SPM (2018)

What we did: Completed a major upgrade of the organization's surgery instrument sterilization system - implemented a mobile app workflow and a bi-directional, real-time HL7 interface as part of the upgrade.
Why a favorite: This was my first experience working on an alpha interface build. There was a lot of tweaking, testing involved, but I learned quite a bit about HL7 behavior by project close.
More on SPM

Omnitool (2020)

What we did: Implemented ability for physicians to make ventilation adjusts on Covid-19 patients from outside of the patient room - reduced physician exposure and PPE.
Why a favorite: This implementation was a tangible opportunity to make a direct impact on a highly-challenging situtation facing our staff.
More on Omnitool

Patient Check-in (2018)

What we did: Revamped the organization's ambulatory clinic patient check-in process by introducing self-service kiosk and mobile device check-in.
Why a favorite: This implementation had it all - technical, operational, business, and politcal challenges. Through it all, however, we were able to implement a solution that was a win-win for both our patients and staff and met the business goals of our organzization.
More on Clearwave

e-Prescription (2019)

What we did: Implemented technology that allowed our physicians to e-prescribe controlled substances.
Why a favorite: The inclusion of DEA requirements on this implementation greatly upped my workflow documentation skill by necessity. It also opened my eyes a bit to to the heavily regulated world of controlled substance Rx writing.
More on Imprivata EPCS

Physician Ribbon

What we did: Integrated an application into our EMR that proactively communicates order behavior pattern information to physicians - intended to standardize ordering patterns and reduce waste.
Why a favorite: This implementation was an opportunity to play a role in ensuring the continued financial health of our organization in a tangible way.
More on IllumiCare Physician Ribbon

CipherRounds

What we did: Worked with our patient experience team to implement a mobile-based application that standardizes patient and staff rounding practices for the organization.
Why a favorite: Beyond the success of the implementation, this was a favorite due to the quality people I had the opportunity to interact with on our patient experience team and at CipherHealth.
More on CipherRounds
